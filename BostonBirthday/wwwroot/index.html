Short Bio

I’m Boston, a composer and multi-instrumentalist who spends most of his time writing music, tinkering with sounds, and drinking far too much coffee. I love blending jazz harmony with electronic textures and turning small melodic ideas into big emotional tracks.

When I’m not making music, you’ll probably find me wandering thrift stores, hunting for weird records, or annoying friends with music theory facts.

About This Track

“City After Midnight” started as a piano loop I recorded on my phone. It grew into a full track with layered horns, analog synths, and chopped-up drum samples. The goal was to capture that feeling of walking home way too late with headphones on.

I’m experimenting with more live trumpet and sax parts in future releases, so expect a lot more horn-heavy music soon.
