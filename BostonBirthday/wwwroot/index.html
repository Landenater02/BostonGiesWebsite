Boston Gies

Boston Gies is a composer, vocalist, and electronic musician. He is interested in working on film, TV, and video game music. Boston loves sculpting worlds with music, whether with electronic or acoustic instrumentation. He is currently composing a comedic, absurd cowboy-western musical titled “The Golden Mule.”
