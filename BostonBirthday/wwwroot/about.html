Fun Bio

I’m Boston Geo – producer, trumpet player, and professional over-thinker of chord progressions. I grew up obsessed with movie soundtracks and video-game music, which slowly mutated into a love for jazz harmony and electronic production.

Most of my tracks start as ideas on a beat-up keyboard or a trumpet line I can’t get out of my head. From there I build layers of drums, synths, and textures until it feels like its own little world.

When I’m not writing or recording, I’m probably at a show, hanging out in a practice room, or mixing for friends. I like collaborators who are curious, a little weird, and not afraid to try something that might fail spectacularly.

Things I’m Interested In

  • Experimental jazz harmony and lush extended chords
  • Combining acoustic instruments with glitchy electronic textures
  • Trumpet, flugelhorn, and brass arrangement
  • Game and film composition – especially moody, atmospheric cues
  • Concept albums built around a story or fictional city
  • Collaborating with singers, rappers, and instrumentalists

I’m especially interested in doing more composition for short films, games, and animation . If that’s your world, definitely reach out.
